About Me

I am Pallika Dhingra, currently pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence from Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women.

During a recent internship, I delved into the fascinating realms of Computer Vision and Deep Learning. One of my notable projects involved using CycleGAN to transform original images into artistic renditions that captured the styles of renowned artists like Van Gogh and Monet.

In addition to my work in AI, I have a strong foundation in Data Structures, primarily practiced in JAVA, and have explored several Python libraries to enhance my technical expertise.
